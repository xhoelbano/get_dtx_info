What to expect

You will work through a total of 6 online course units, all based on proven cognitive-behavioural therapy strategies. The course units each take around an hour to complete and are easy to fit around your daily life. 4 weeks after the end of the course, you will complete the programme with a refresher unit.

Our vivid and effective exercises will help you to lastingly improve your mood, strengthen your problem-solving skills, find helpful ways to deal with troublesome thoughts or worries, and much more.

This course is suitable for you if...

  • you suffer from diabetes (type 1 or type 2) and want to improve your quality of life
  • you’re feeling down or sad, you would like to increase your wellbeing and lust for life
  • your thoughts revolve around your health situation and possible secondary conditions
  • you’re experiencing a lack of interest and drive and want to tackle these symptoms

How our online course works

Our effective online courses are carefully structured and combined with useful features to give you the best possible support.

  • Course overview A total of 6 online course units, each lasting around an hour, that you can easily fit around your daily life.
  • Optional modules As well as the fixed course units, you can choose to complete two optional modules.
  • Diary Keep track of your mood in your mood diary and get feedback on your entries.
  • Example content With texts, videos, audio content and exercises, the course holds plenty of variety, as well as being effective.

Strengthen your wellbeing with HelloBetter

Feeling down, losing interest, lacking drive, ruminating – suffering from depressive symptoms is tough and can have a major impact on your life. Worries or burdens that stem from diabetes can equally affect your quality of life.

In a randomised controlled trial, the HelloBetter course was shown to reduce depressive symptoms and emotional burdens related to diabetes. What’s more, the trial participants were still experiencing the positive effects 6 months after the end of the course.


In line with your own needs, you can learn in more depth about:

  • establishing healthy sleep habits
  • strengthening your partnership and dealing with emotional strain
  • integrating healthy eating habits into your everyday life

With the different methods you learn:

  • to noticeably improve your mood
  • to plan more positive activities in your everyday life
  • to be more active in your everyday life
  • to solve your problems step by step
  • to deal with rumination or worries due to diabetes
  • to engage in trusting dialogue with your doctor

Proven to reduce depressive symptoms and emotional burdens

The effectiveness of the online course "HelloBetter Diabetes" has been scientifically verified.

  • The study participants experienced a reduction in depressive symptoms and in emotional burdens associated with diabetes.
  • These positive effects were sustained over the long term and could still be observed six months after completion of the course.
  • 95% of the study participants would recommend the course to others.

Effectiveness of the online course

The effectiveness of our "HelloBetter Diabetes" course has been proven in these two clinical studies:

  • 2015
    Efficacy of a web-based intervention with mobile phone support in treating depressive symptoms in adults with type 1 and type 2 diabetes: a randomized controlled trial.
  • 2015
    The 6 month effectiveness of Internet based guided self help for depression in adults with Type1 and 2 diabetes mellitus

Our Fit im Stress Training bears the seal of the Central Test Centre for Prevention (ZPP) and is therefore partially reimbursed by almost all statutory health insurance companies.
The costs are covered by reimbursement procedures. This means that you have to pay the course fees yourself first. After the course, you can submit a confirmation of participation to your health insurance company to get the costs reimbursed. The prerequisite is that you have completed 100% of the course.

After the end of the course, you can easily apply for your confirmation of participation at support@hellobetter.de.
